Welcome to the Cloud Cover Study

In this study, you will be viewing a series of short animated visualizations that show cloud cover over time. Each animation lasts approximately 7.2 seconds and will loop continuously while you're on the page.

How to Read the Animations:

White areas indicate more cloud cover
Blue areas indicate less or no cloud cover

What You Will Be Asked:

After watching each animation, you will answer one question. There are three possible types of questions:

Quadrant selection: Choose the quadrant (Top Left, Top Right, Bottom Left, Bottom Right) that best answers the question.
Direction selection: Identify the direction the clouds are moving (Upwards, Downwards, Leftwards, Rightwards).
Click selection: Click on the part of the animation that best answers the question.
